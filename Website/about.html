About VIC

Our Mission

Valiance Insurance Company (VIC) is dedicated to helping policyholders unlock the hidden value in their life insurance policies. We believe that your life insurance should work for you - not against you. When life circumstances change, we provide a dignified and profitable alternative to letting your policy lapse or accepting a low cash surrender value.

Our Approach

At VIC, we leverage cutting-edge actuarial models and life expectancy data to provide fair, competitive valuations for your life insurance policy. Our pricing methodology is based on industry-standard VBT2015 mortality tables, enhanced with proprietary analytical techniques to ensure you receive the best possible offer.

Key Principles:

  • Fair Valuations: We use advanced analytics and actuarial models to determine the true market value of your policy
  • Customer First: Your financial wellbeing and peace of mind drive everything we do
  • Transparency: We explain exactly how we value your policy with clear, honest communication
  • Fast Process: We streamline the evaluation and payment process to get you cash quickly
  • Expertise: Our team has decades of experience in life settlements and actuarial science

Our Valuation Technology

VIC employs sophisticated pricing models that accurately determine your policy's market value by incorporating:

  • VBT2015 mortality tables and life expectancy calculations
  • Advanced statistical analysis of policy performance and market trends
  • Real-time data processing for fast, accurate valuations
  • Secure, cloud-based infrastructure protecting your personal information
  • Market intelligence on institutional buyer demand for life settlements

Experience
