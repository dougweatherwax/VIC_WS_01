About VIC

Our Mission

Valiance Insurance Company (VIC) is dedicated to providing innovative, data-driven insurance solutions that protect what matters most. We combine advanced actuarial science with personalized service to deliver products that meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Our Approach

At VIC, we leverage cutting-edge pricing models and mortality data to ensure our products are both competitive and sustainable. Our pricing methodology is based on industry-standard VBT2015 mortality tables, enhanced with proprietary analytical techniques.

Key Principles:

  • Data-Driven Decisions: We use advanced analytics and actuarial models to price our products fairly and accurately
  • Customer First: Your needs and goals drive everything we do
  • Transparency: We believe in clear, honest communication about our products and pricing
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our models and processes to serve you better
  • Sustainability: Our pricing ensures long-term viability for both our company and your coverage

Our Technology

VIC employs sophisticated pricing models that incorporate:

  • VBT2015 mortality tables for accurate risk assessment
  • Advanced statistical analysis for pricing optimization
  • Real-time data processing for efficient quote generation
  • Secure, cloud-based infrastructure for reliable service delivery

Experience

Our team brings decades of combined experience in actuarial science, insurance, and financial services.

Reliability

Built on robust technology and sound actuarial principles, our products provide the security you can count on.

Support

We're here when you need us, with dedicated support to help you make informed decisions.
